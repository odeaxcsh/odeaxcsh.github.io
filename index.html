Research

My research career is pretty new but we are at the process of publishing following paper:

  • PCLIPPER+: A Robust Point Cloud Registration Algorithm in Structured Environments

    In this work, we improved point cloud registration by removing repetitive patterns. We split the point cloud into planes and points, then use a graph-based method to find the best transformation. Our results showed a better balance of speed and accuracy compared to SOTA.
