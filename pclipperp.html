PCLIPPER+: A Robust Point Cloud Registration Algorithm in Structured Environments

Computer Vision Point Cloud Registration

Abstract

In this work, we improved point cloud registration by removing repetitive patterns. We split the point cloud into planes and points, then use a graph-based method to find the best transformation. Our results showed a better balance of speed and accuracy compared to state-of-the-art methods.

Method

Our approach addresses the challenges of point cloud registration in structured environments by:

  • Identifying and removing repetitive geometric patterns that can lead to false correspondences
  • Decomposing point clouds into planar and non-planar components for more robust matching
  • Employing graph-based optimization to find optimal transformations
  • Balancing computational efficiency with registration accuracy

Results

Experimental validation demonstrates that PCLIPPER+ achieves superior performance compared to existing methods, particularly in environments with repetitive structures such as urban scenes and indoor spaces.
